Apple Sudden Motion Producer

1. Summary
Apple MacBook Pro includes a 3-axis accelerometer (X Y Z).
This bundle provides a WireAdmin producer services pushing a array of Measurement for the 3 acceleration values (in m/s2) from the Mac' Sudden Motion sensor.

2. Contributors
Arnaud Constancin, Lionel Touseau, Mikael Desertot

3. Dependencies
This bundle uses
* the SMS JNI lib from http://www.shiffman.net/p5/sms/
* and the UniMotion 0.4.1 (released under the LGPL) http://unimotion.sourceforge.net/
