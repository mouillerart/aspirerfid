zigbee.devices

1. Summary
The bundle provides services to discover ZigBee devices and to send/receive raw packets with them.
It provides also a command for the Felix shell.

2. Authors
Lionel Touseau, Didier Donsez

3. Tested devices
* SunSPOT(tm) base station and sensors
* more are welcomed
